Copyright Notice

GujaratExamHub respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects users to do the same. We will respond to valid copyright infringement notices in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

1. Our Policy

GujaratExamHub does not host any copyrighted content on our servers. We only provide links to publicly available materials hosted on third-party platforms like Google Drive. However, we take copyright infringement seriously and will:

  • Promptly respond to valid DMCA takedown notices
  • Remove links to allegedly infringing content
  • Take appropriate action against repeat infringers
  • Respect the rights of content creators

2. Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed, please provide our designated agent with a written notice that includes:

Required Information:

  • Your Contact Information: Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Identification of Copyrighted Work: A description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed
  • Location of Infringing Material: The specific URL or page on our website where the infringing link appears
  • Good Faith Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  • Accuracy Statement: A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf
  • Physical or Electronic Signature: Your physical or electronic signature

3. How to Submit a Notice

Send your DMCA takedown notice to:

DMCA Designated Agent

  • Website: gujaratexamhub.github.io
  • Contact Form: Submit via Contact Page

Subject Line: "DMCA Takedown Request - [Your Name/Organization]"

4. Response Timeline

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Acknowledge receipt within 24-48 hours
  • Review the notice for completeness and validity
  • Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content within 72 hours
  • Notify you once the action has been taken

5. Counter-Notification

If you believe that your content was removed or disabled by mistake or misidentification, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email
  • Identification of the material that was removed
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that the material was removed by mistake
  • Your consent to jurisdiction of the federal court in your district
  • Your physical or electronic signature

6. Misrepresentation Warning

⚠️ Important Notice

Under Section 512(f) of the DMCA, anyone who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing or was removed by mistake may be subject to liability. Please ensure your claim is accurate before submitting.

7. Repeat Infringer Policy

We maintain a policy of terminating access for users or content sources that are repeat infringers of copyright. If we receive multiple valid DMCA notices regarding the same source, we will take appropriate action.

8. Educational Fair Use

Please note that many materials on our website may be protected under fair use provisions for educational purposes. Before submitting a DMCA notice, please consider whether the use qualifies as fair use under copyright law.
